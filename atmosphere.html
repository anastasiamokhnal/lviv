First of all this is not about touristic places that you can easily find in the net (but they are also amazing). It is about atmosphere that you must feel because Lviv isn't run-of-the-mill place. One of the things that everyone likes is street musicians. You won't be able steer clear them and you shouldn't. Just dance or sing along with them

Old city with it's own beauty. Buildings and streets that will caught your eye. there is a street with several names: as of 2013, Archivnaya Street was named after Bergman, Fellini, Truffaut, Ilyenko, Parajanov and Tarkovsky. Get unforgettable experience. You won't regret

Walking up and down the streets you can only imagine how much events those walls have seen. Everything around you is breathing history. It is alive past that you can touch. By the way, due to the similarity with the capital of France, it was in Lviv that M. Boyarsky's film "The Three Musketeers" was once shot.
